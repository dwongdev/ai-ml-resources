### LLMs

- [OpenAI LLMs](https://openai.com/product/gpt-4) ‚≠ê - The best LLMs, in particular GPT-4. Also features GPT-3.5 and other models. GPT-3.5 and GPT-4 are used in ChatGPT.
- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/) ‚≠ê - The leading open-source AI community. Here you can find not only the trending models but also datasets and spaces (instances of models running).
